# Email snippet — L01: EU AI Act and legal AI

**Subject:** The EU AI Act is already in force. Here's what it actually requires from your practice.

**Preview text:** Almost everything written about the AI Act confuses provider obligations with deployer obligations. The difference is €330,000 in compliance costs vs. essentially zero.

---

The EU AI Act entered into force on 1 August 2024. AI literacy obligations are active since February 2025. Full enforcement begins August 2026.

But if you're a tax professional *using* AI tools (a deployer), not building them (a provider), your obligations are minimal: train your staff, maintain human oversight, keep logs, follow instructions. No conformity assessment. No quality management system.

We wrote a practical guide covering the exact timeline, the high-risk classification question for legal AI, Belgian-specific implementation (BIPT, OVB, ITAA positions), and a compliance checklist.

The key insight: the AI Act doesn't ban AI in professional practice. It bans *bad* AI in professional practice.
