**Subject:** When your AI tool hides the disagreement
**Preview:** The Belgian Supreme Court contradicted itself on the same doctrine. Would your AI tool tell you?

In October 2023, the Belgian Supreme Court ruled that foreign income is exempt from Belgian tax under the scope principle. One year later, the same court ruled the opposite — requiring effective taxation. Same doctrine, opposite conclusions.

Most AI tools would give you one answer confidently. They would not mention the other ruling. That confident silence is more dangerous than a wrong answer — because it doesn't trigger verification.

We think professionals deserve to see the full contradiction surface. When sources disagree, the system should tell you, not decide for you.
