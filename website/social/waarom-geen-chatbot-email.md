**Subject:** Your AI tool produces answers you can't defend. Here's the alternative.

**Preview:** Chat conversations are ephemeral. Professional advice must be defensible. The paradigm is wrong.

---

Your client asks how you reached your tax position from six weeks ago. You open your AI tool and scroll through a chat history. Good luck finding that exchange — if the session wasn't deleted.

The chatbot paradigm has a structural problem. Not accuracy — ephemerality. Chat conversations are unstructured, unreproducible, unexportable, and indefensible. Since mid-2023, over 300 cases of AI-generated legal hallucinations have been documented. Most involved professionals who treated chat output as research.
