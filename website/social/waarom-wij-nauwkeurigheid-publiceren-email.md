**Subject:** Why we publish our accuracy — and what it means for your practice
**Preview:** Every legal AI vendor claims high accuracy. Almost none will show you the data.

Every legal AI vendor promises high accuracy. Stanford researchers tested the leading tools and found hallucination rates of 17-33% — despite "hallucination-free" marketing claims.

We think professionals who stake their reputation on AI-assisted research deserve to see the report card. That's why we publish our accuracy metrics continuously — citation accuracy, substantive correctness, and cross-domain completeness.

When the law changes and our accuracy dips, you'll see it. When a user reports an error, it becomes a permanent test case. Transparency isn't a marketing gesture. It's an improvement engine.
