## Definition

Faithfulness is an evaluation criterion asking whether an explanation or [chain-of-thought](/en/glossary/chain-of-thought/) truly corresponds to how the model arrived at its [prediction](/en/glossary/inference/), rather than being a plausible but disconnected story.
