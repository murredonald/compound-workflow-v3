AI in tax research: myths vs. reality
Myth
"ChatGPT can replace a tax advisor — it knows all the laws."
Reality
General LLMs hallucinate 58–88% of the time on legal questions and cannot cite sources.
Myth
"If the AI sounds confident, the answer must be correct."
Reality
LLMs express equal confidence for correct and incorrect answers. Confidence scoring requires specialized architecture.
Myth
"RAG eliminates all AI errors."
Reality
RAG reduces hallucinations to 17–33% — a major improvement, but professional verification remains essential.
